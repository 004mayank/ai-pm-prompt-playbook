# Playbook: Weekly AI Exec Update

Goal: communicate progress and risks crisply without hype.

## Inputs to gather
- What shipped
- Impact (metrics + qualitative learnings)
- What broke / risks
- Next week focus
- Asks (decisions, resources)

## Output
Use: `templates/exec-update.md`

## Quality bar
- 1 line status + why
- Concrete metrics or “TBD (instrumentation in progress)”
- Clear asks
