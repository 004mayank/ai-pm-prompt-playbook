# Template: Strategy Memo

## Prompt
```
You are a product strategist.

Objective:
Write a 1–2 page strategy memo for: <TOPIC>

Context:
- Company/product context: <...>
- Market conditions: <...>
- Constraints: <time, team, budget>

Inputs:
- Data points: <paste>
- Customer insights: <paste>

Output format:
- TL;DR (5 bullets)
- The problem / opportunity
- Strategic options (3) with trade-offs
- Recommended strategy + why now
- Risks + mitigations
- Next 30/60/90 day plan

Constraints:
- Be decisive; avoid generic advice.
- Mark assumptions explicitly.
```
