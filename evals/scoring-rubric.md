# Evals: Scoring Rubric (0–2 each)

Use this to score prompts/templates and the outputs they produce.

## 1) Clarity of objective
- 0: vague
- 1: somewhat clear
- 2: unambiguous, decision-oriented

## 2) Grounded inputs
- 0: no inputs, invites guessing
- 1: some inputs, gaps not handled
- 2: inputs included + unknowns marked

## 3) Constraints / guardrails
- 0: none
- 1: present but generic
- 2: explicit NEVER/ALWAYS + failure modes

## 4) Output format
- 0: freeform
- 1: basic structure
- 2: strict headings/schema, easy to compare

## 5) Decision usefulness
- 0: fluffy
- 1: partially actionable
- 2: crisp choices, trade-offs, next steps

## 6) Safety / correctness
- 0: hallucination risk high
- 1: some checks
- 2: asks questions when needed, separates facts vs assumptions

### Interpreting totals
- 0–5: unusable
- 6–8: decent first draft
- 9–11: strong
- 12: production-grade
